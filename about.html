About

A family business rooted in simplicity, craftsmanship, and faith.

Our Story

First Light started with our family's own desire to learn how to nourish our bodies with clean and natural ingredients in food and in products we use on our bodies and around the house.

I started having autoimmune issues in 2021 and we started down the rabbit hole of living a clean and organic lifestyle. We began with food and have slowly branched out into learning how to make other household essentials.

Our skin was often dry and my daughter was constantly dealing with itchy, dry, and scaly patches on her hands. I often bought thick lotions that had a long list of ingredients. A friend told me about tallow balm, so I did some research and decided to make it for our family.

We were so thrilled when we saw the results that tallow balm had on our skin. It completely healed my daughter's skin issues in less than a week. My hands, elbows, knees, and heels have never been so soft in my life. We use pure and simple ingredients that I am happy to use on my own children!

Friends and family have all raved about how much they enjoy the balm, so we decided to make this available for more people to enjoy and benefit from. Our desire is truly to make a product you will find healing and nourishing to your body.

Why "First Light"

Our company name is inspired by imagery from the Bible of light in the darkness.

Jesus is described as the Sunrise from on high who visited us to shine His light on a dark world. He brings immense hope and peace to individuals living in a world that can be filled with pain and despair. There is comfort and hope. He was the First Light.

Luke 1:78-79
Because of the tender mercy of our God,
With which the Sunrise from on high will visit us,
To shine on those who sit in darkness and the shadow of death,
To guide our feet into the way of peace.

We don't see skincare as merely another product on a shelf. It is a daily ritual, a quiet moment to slow down, care for yourself, and appreciate the goodness of God's creation.

Every jar is handcrafted with that same spirit: simple, honest, and made with purpose.

Our Philosophy

These are the beliefs that shape every decision we make, from what goes into our balm to how we run our business.

  • God made the natural ingredients our skin needs most.
  • Fewer, better ingredients outperform long ingredient lists.
  • Small-batch craftsmanship creates better products.
  • Quality begins with thoughtful sourcing.
  • Caring for your skin can be both simple and meaningful.
