About

Our Story

First Light started in our kitchen.

My wife Tatiana was looking for something that would genuinely work on our skin. We live in Boise's high desert, where the air dries you out fast and most moisturizers just can't keep up. She wanted something simple and real, made with ingredients she could actually trust.

So she made her own.

After researching traditional skincare ingredients, she landed on tallow. Grass-fed beef tallow has a fatty acid profile remarkably similar to the oils our skin naturally produces. It is rich in fat-soluble vitamins. People used it for generations before modern skincare moved toward more complicated formulas.

The first batch was for our family. The reaction was immediate. The texture was unlike anything we had tried. It absorbed differently. It felt different. By the next morning, skin that had been chronically dry looked genuinely better.

We shared it with friends. Then friends of friends. The feedback was the same every time: I have never felt anything like this. People started asking how to get more.

Then something surprised us. A local store asked to carry it. Another called the very next morning. Within two days, two shops had agreed to stock First Light. What had started as a kitchen project for our family had become something worth sharing more widely.

We named the business First Light after Luke 1:78, which calls Jesus the "Sunrise from on high." This name is about him. He is the light that shines in the darkness, and he shone into our hearts. We wanted everything we built to carry that meaning.

We are still based in Boise. We still make every jar by hand as a family. The formula has not changed. The same five ingredients from that first batch are in every jar we make today.

Why "First Light"

Our name comes from one of our favorite passages:

"Because of the tender mercy of our God, by which the sunrise shall visit us from on high to give light to those who sit in darkness and in the shadow of death, to guide our feet into the way of peace." Luke 1:78-79

The "Sunrise from on high" in this passage is Jesus. This name is about him. He is the light that shines in the darkness.

We don't see skincare as merely another product on a shelf. It is a daily ritual, a quiet moment to slow down, care for yourself, and appreciate the goodness of God's creation.

Every jar is handcrafted with that same spirit: simple, honest, and made with purpose.

Our Philosophy

These are the beliefs that shape every decision we make, from what goes into our balm to how we run our business.

  • God made the natural ingredients our skin needs most.
  • Fewer, better ingredients outperform long ingredient lists.
  • Small-batch craftsmanship creates better products.
  • Quality begins with thoughtful sourcing.
  • Caring for your skin can be both simple and meaningful.
