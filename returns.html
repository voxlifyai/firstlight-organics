Returns & Refunds

Our Promise

We stand behind every jar we make. If you are not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, reach out to us within 30 days of delivery and we will make it right.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, items must be unused and in their original, unopened packaging. Because our products are applied to skin, we cannot accept returns of opened or partially used items for hygiene reasons.

If your item arrived damaged or defective, please contact us with a photo of the damage and we will send a replacement at no cost to you.

How to Start a Return

Email us at hello@firstlight-organics.com within 30 days of your delivery date. Include your order number and the reason for your return. We will reply within one business day with next steps.

Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item arrived damaged or we made an error on your order. We recommend using a trackable shipping method, as we cannot process refunds for items that do not reach us.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will notify you by email. Approved refunds are issued to your original payment method within 5 to 7 business days. Your bank or card issuer may require additional time to post the credit.

Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges at this time. If you would like a different scent, return the original item and place a new order.

Questions

Reach us at hello@firstlight-organics.com. We are a small family business and personally read every message.
