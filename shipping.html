Shipping Policy

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1 to 3 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays ship on the next business day. You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order has been dispatched.

Shipping Options

We ship within the contiguous United States via USPS.

  • Standard Shipping (3 to 5 business days): $8.00
  • Free standard shipping on orders of $70 or more

International Shipping

We do not offer international shipping at this time. We ship to all 50 U.S. states, though expedited options may not be available for Alaska and Hawaii.

Delays

Delivery estimates are not guaranteed. Carrier delays, weather events, and peak seasons can affect transit times. If your package has not arrived within 10 business days of your ship date, please contact us at hello@firstlight-organics.com and we will look into it.

Lost or Stolen Packages

First Light Organics is not responsible for packages that are lost or stolen after a confirmed delivery scan. If this happens, please contact your local post office and file a claim. We are happy to assist where we can.

Questions

Reach us at hello@firstlight-organics.com. We respond within one business day.
